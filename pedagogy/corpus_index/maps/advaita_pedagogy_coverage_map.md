# Advaita Pedagogy Coverage Map (AIM Corpus)

## Purpose

This document visualizes which **Advaita teaching methods (prakriyās)**
are already represented in the AIM corpus and which major pedagogical
methods remain to be ingested.

The goal is to guide **strategic corpus expansion** so that the AIM
system eventually contains the full classical toolkit of Advaita
pedagogy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Major Pedagogical Methods Still Missing

## 1 Identity Revelation Method

Text: **Chāndogya Upaniṣad**

Key teaching: tat tvam asi

Pedagogical role: Direct revelation of the identity between the
individual Self and Brahman.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2 Radical Negation Method

Text: **Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad**

Key teaching: neti neti

Pedagogical role: Systematic removal of all conceptual identifications.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3 Question-Based Pedagogy

Text: **Praśna Upaniṣad**

Pedagogical role: Structured inquiry through dialogue and questioning.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4 Devotional Nondualism

Text: **Śvetāśvatara Upaniṣad**

Pedagogical role: Integration of Īśvara devotion with nondual
metaphysics.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Strategic Next Ingestion Order

Recommended sequence:

1.  Chāndogya Upaniṣad
2.  Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad
3.  Praśna Upaniṣad
4.  Śvetāśvatara Upaniṣad

After these are added, the AIM corpus will contain **all major classical
Advaita teaching strategies**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Interpretation

The current corpus already provides a strong foundation covering:

-   cosmological projection
-   phenomenological reduction
-   consciousness analysis
-   existential inquiry
-   renunciation
-   knowledge hierarchy

These methods already allow the AIM system to respond to many forms of
student confusion.

The remaining texts primarily expand **identity teaching and negation
methods**, which complete the Advaita pedagogical toolkit.
