# Advaita Corpus Teaching Map

This document maps major Advaita Vedānta texts onto the **Universal
Advaita Teaching Progression**. The goal is to show how different
scriptures participate in a coordinated pedagogical structure.

Rather than presenting isolated philosophies, the Advaita corpus behaves
like a **layered teaching ecosystem**. Different texts specialize in
different stages of the teaching process.

# Example Teaching Trajectory Across Texts

A classical Advaita curriculum might follow this trajectory:

Kaṭha 1.1\
→ existential shock (death confrontation)

Kaṭha 1.2\
→ śreyas vs preyas discrimination

Kaṭha 1.3\
→ instrument analysis (chariot metaphor)

Aitareya / Chāndogya\
→ ontological inquiry (cause‑effect reasoning)

Taittirīya\
→ progressive negation (five sheaths)

Māṇḍūkya\
→ witness recognition (analysis of waking, dream, sleep)

Chāndogya 6\
→ **tat tvam asi** identity teaching

Bṛhadāraṇyaka\
→ **neti neti** non‑objectifiability clarification

Upadeśa‑sāhasrī\
→ stabilization and assimilation
