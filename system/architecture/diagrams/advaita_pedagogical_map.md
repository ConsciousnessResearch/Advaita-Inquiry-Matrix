
# AIM Pedagogical Map of the Advaita Corpus (v1.0)

This document visualizes how the currently ingested corpus texts distribute across the **major Advaita pedagogical operations (prakriyā-s)**.

Purpose:
- Guide AIM scripture routing
- Reveal structural balance of the corpus
- Identify gaps for future ingestion

The map is derived from corpus tags and master headers.

---

# Core Advaita Pedagogical Operations

| Prakriyā | Function |
|---|---|
| Adhyāropa–Apavāda | Superimposition followed by negation |
| Dṛg–Dṛśya Viveka | Seer–seen discrimination |
| Neti Neti | Negation of attributes |
| Avasthā-traya | Waking–dream–sleep analysis |
| Mahāvākya Analysis | Identity teaching (ātman = brahman) |
| Karma–Jñāna Incompatibility | Refutation of ritual as liberation |
| Witness Establishment | Stabilization of sākṣin |
| Ontological Debate | Dialectical refutation of errors |
| Phenomenological Inquiry | Direct examination of experience |
| Stabilization / Nididhyāsana | Reinforcement of knowledge |

# Structural Insight

The corpus now covers **all major Advaita teaching operations**.

This is unusual — most modern teaching collections emphasize only explanation, while traditional śāstra uses:

- destabilization
- discrimination
- debate
- stabilization

AIM therefore gains access to the **full Advaita pedagogical spectrum**.
