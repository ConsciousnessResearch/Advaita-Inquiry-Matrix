> > ## **Mantra 1**
> >
> > 
> >
> > 
> >
> > **Sanskrit**
> >
> > 
> >
> > ॐ ईशावास्यमिदं सर्वं यत्किञ्च जगत्यां जगत् ।
> >
> > तेन त्यक्तेन भुञ्जीथा मा गृधः कस्य स्विद्धनम् ॥ १ ॥
> >
> > 
> >
> > **Transliteration**
> >
> > 
> >
> > oṁ īśā vāsyam idaṁ sarvaṁ yat kiñca jagatyāṁ jagat |
> >
> > tena tyaktena bhuñjīthā mā gṛdhaḥ kasya svid dhanam || 1 ||
> >
> > 
> >
> > **Translation**
> >
> > 
> >
> > All this—whatever moves in this world—is pervaded by the Lord. By renunciation, enjoy; do not covet the wealth of anyone.
