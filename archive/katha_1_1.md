### Mantra 1

**Sanskrit**

ओं सहनावितिशान्तिः । ओं उशन्ह वै वाजश्रवसः सर्ववेदसंददौ । तस्य ह नचिकेता
नाम पुत्र आस ॥ १ ॥

**Transliteration**

oṃ sahanāvitiśāntiḥ \| oṃ uśanha vai vājaśravasaḥ sarvavedasaṃdadau \|
tasya ha naciketā nāma putra āsa \|\| 1 \|\|

**Translation**

From desire, as story tells, the son of Vajasrava made a gift of all his
wealth; he had a son, so the story goes, by name Nachiketas.

### Mantra 3

**Sanskrit**

पीतोदका जग्धतृणा दुग्धदोहा निरिन्द्रियाः । अनन्दा नाम ते लोकास्तान्स
गच्छति ता ददत् ॥ ३ ॥

**Transliteration**

pītodakā jagdhatṛṇā dugdhadohā nirindriyāḥ \| anandā nāma te lokāstānsa
gacchati tā dadat \|\| 3 \|\|

**Translation**

(These cows) have drunk water for the last time, eaten grass for the
last time, have yielded all their milk and are devoid of vigour. Joyless
verily are those worlds; them he attains who gives these.

### Mantra 4

**Sanskrit**

स होवाच पितरं तत कस्मै मां दास्यसीति । द्वितीयं तृतीयं तँ होवाच मृत्यवे
त्वा ददामीति ॥ ४ ॥

**Transliteration**

sa hovāca pitaraṃ tata kasmai māṃ dāsyasīti \| dvitīyaṃ tṛtīyaṃ tam̐
hovāca mṛtyave tvā dadāmīti \|\| 4 \|\|

**Translation**

He said unto his sire, 'father, to whom wilt thou give me'; he said this
again and for the third time. To him, he said 'unto Death do I give
thee.'

### Mantra 5

**Sanskrit**

बहूनामेमि प्रथमो बहूनामेमि मध्यमः । किँ स्विद्यमस्य कर्तव्यं यन्मयाद्य
करिष्यति ॥ ५ ॥

**Transliteration**

bahūnāmemi prathamo bahūnāmemi madhyamaḥ \| kim̐ svidyamasya kartavyaṃ
yanmayādya kariṣyati \|\| 5 \|\|

**Translation**

Of many I go the first; of many I go midmost; what is there for Death to
do which he can now do by me?

### Mantra 6

**Sanskrit**

अनुपश्य यथा पूर्वे प्रतिपश्य तथापरे । सस्यमिव मर्त्यः पच्यते
सस्यमिवाजायते पुनः ॥ ६ ॥

**Transliteration**

anupaśya yathā pūrve pratipaśya tathāpare \| sasyamiva martyaḥ pacyate
sasyamivājāyate punaḥ \|\| 6 \|\|

**Translation**

Call to mind how our ancestors behaved and mark also how others now
behave; like corn, decays the mortal and like corn is born again.
