## Text 124

**Sanskrit**

अथ ते संप्रवक्ष्यािम स्वरूपं परमात्मनः ।
यिद्वज्ञाय नरो बन्धान्मुक्तः कै वल्यमश्नुते ॥ १२४ ॥

**Transliteration**

atha te saṃpravakṣyāmi svarūpaṃ paramātmanaḥ |
yadvijñāya naro bandhānmuktaḥ kaivalyamaśnute || 124 ||

**Translation**

“I shall now tell you clearly the nature of the Paramātman, by knowing which a man is freed from all bonds and attains liberation.”
