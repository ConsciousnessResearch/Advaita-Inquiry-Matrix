## Text 36

**Sanskrit**

शान्ता महान्तो िनवसिन्त सन्तो
वसन्तवल्लोकिहतं चरन्तः ।
तीणार्ः स्वयं भीमभवाणर्वं जनान्
अहेतुनान् यानिप तारयन्तः ॥ ३७ ॥

**Transliteration**

durvārasaṃsāradavāgnitaptaṃ
dodhūyamānaṃ duradṛṣṭavātaiḥ |
bhītaṃ prapannaṃ paripāhi mṛtyoḥ
śaraṇyamanyadyadahaṃ na jāne || 36 ||

**Translation**

“Save me from death: afﬂicted as I am by the unquenchable forest-ﬁre of this world, and thrown violently by the cruel storms of misfortune; terriﬁed and seeking refuge in Thee, for I do not know of any other man with whom to seek shelter.”
