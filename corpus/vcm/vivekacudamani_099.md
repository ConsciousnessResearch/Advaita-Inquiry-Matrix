## Text 99

**Sanskrit**

कत्रार्िदभावं प्रितपद्य राजते यत्र स्वयं भाित ह्ययं परात्मा ।
धीमात्रकोपािधरशेषसाक्षी न िलप्यते तत्कृ तकमर्लेशैः
यस्मादसङ्गस्तत एव कमर्िभः
न िलप्यते िकं िचदुपािधना कृ तैः ॥ ९९ ॥

**Transliteration**

kartrādibhāvaṃ pratipadya rājate
yatra svayaṃ bhāti hyayaṃ parātmā |
dhīmātrakopādhiraśeṣasākṣīna na lipyate tatkṛtakarmaleśaiḥ
yasmādasaṅgastata eva karmabhiḥ
na lipyate kiṃcidupādhinā kṛtaiḥ || 99 ||

**Translation**

“In this dreaming condition, the supreme Self shines in its own glory, with the intellect as its only limitation—the witness of everything,  uncontaminated in the least by the activities of the intellect. Since it is entirely unattached, it is not tainted by any action that its apparent conditioning may perform.“
