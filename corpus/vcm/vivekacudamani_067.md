## Text 67

**Sanskrit**

यस्त्वयाद्य कृ तः प्रश्नो वरीयाञ्ज्छास्त्रिवन्मतः ।
सूत्रप्रायो िनगूढाथोर् ज्ञातव्यश्च मुमुक्षुिभः ॥ ६७ ॥

**Transliteration**

yastvayādya kṛtaḥ praśno varīyāñjchāstravinmataḥ |
sūtraprāyo nigūḍhārtho jñātavyaśca mumukṣubhiḥ || 67 ||

**Translation**

“The questions you have raised today are excellent, accepted by those well-versed in the scriptures, aphoristic, full of hidden meaning and are ﬁt to be known by all seekers of liberation.”
