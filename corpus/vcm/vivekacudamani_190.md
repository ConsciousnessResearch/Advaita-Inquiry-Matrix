## Text 190

**Sanskrit**

स्वयं पिरच्छेदमुपेत्य बुद्धेः
तादात्म्यदोषेण परं मृषात्मनः ।
सवार्त्मकः सन्निप वीक्षते स्वयं
स्वतः पृथक्त्वेन मृदो घटािनव ॥ १९० ॥

**Transliteration**

svayaṃ paricchedamupetya buddheḥ
tādātmyadoṣeṇa paraṃ mṛṣātmanaḥ |
sarvātmakaḥ sannapi vīkṣate svayaṃ
svataḥ pṛthaktvena mṛdo ghaṭāniva || 190 ||

**Translation**

“This Ātman, although the Self of all, by mistaken identiﬁcation with the intellect, assumes the limitations of the intellect, considering Itself as different—like pots thinking they are different from the clay of which they are made.“
