## Text 52

**Sanskrit**

मस्तकन्यस्तभारादेदु
र्ःखमन्यैिनर् वायर्ते ।
क्षुधािदकृ तदुःखं तु िवना स्वेन न के निचत् ॥ ५२ ॥

**Transliteration**

mastakanyastabhārāderduḥkhamanyairnivāryate |
kṣudhādikṛtaduḥkhaṃ tu vinā svena na kenacit || 52 ||

**Translation**

“Exhaustion and fatigue caused by carrying a load on the head, etc. can be relieved by others. But none save one‘s own self can end the pangs caused by hunger, etc.”
