## Text 130

**Sanskrit**

अहङ्कारािददेहान्ता िवषयाश्च सुखादयः ।
वेद्यन्ते घटवद्येन िनत्यबोधस्वरूिपणा ॥ १३० ॥

**Transliteration**

ahaṅkārādidehāntā viṣayāśca sukhādayaḥ |
vedyante ghaṭavadyena nityabodhasvarūpiṇā || 130 ||

**Translation**

“That by which, being of the nature of eternal Knowledge, everything from ahaṃkāra to the physical body, including sense objects, pleasure etc., is known like a pot—that is the Ātman.
