## Text 71

**Sanskrit**

यद्बोद्धव्यं तवेदानीमात्मानात्मिववेचनम् ।
तदुच्यते मया सम्यक्श्रुत्वात्मन्यवधारय ॥ ७१ ॥

**Transliteration**

yadboddhavyaṃ tavedānīmātmānātmavivecanam |
taducyate mayā samyakśrutvātmanyavadhāraya || 71 ||

**Translation**

“Now I am going to describe the discrimination between the Self and not-Self most elaborately. It is what you ought to know. Listen to it properly and then decide about it well in your mind.”
