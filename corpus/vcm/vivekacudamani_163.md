## Text 163

**Sanskrit**

देहात्मधीरेव नृणामसिद्धयां
जन्मािददुःखप्रभवस्य बीजम् ।
यतस्ततस्त्वं जिह तां प्रयत्नात्
त्यक्ते तु िचत्ते न पुनभर्वाशा ॥ १६४ ॥

**Transliteration**

chāyāśarīre pratibimbagātre
yatsvapnadehe hṛdi kalpitāṅge |
yathātmabuddhistava nāsti kācij
jīvaccharīre ca tathaiva māstu || 163 ||

**Translation**

“Just as you do not identify ourself with your shadow of your body, in its reﬂection in a mirror, when it is seen in a dream, or when you imagine it in your mind, so too you should not identify yourself with your living body.“
