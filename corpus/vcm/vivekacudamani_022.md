## Text 22

**Sanskrit**

िवरज्य िवषयव्राताद्दोषदृष्ट्या मुहुमु
र्हुः ।
स्वलक्ष्ये िनयतावस्था मनसः शम उच्यते ॥ २२ ॥

**Transliteration**

virajya viṣayavrātāddoṣadṛṣṭyā muhurmuhuḥ |
svalakṣye niyatāvasthā manasaḥ śama ucyate || 22 ||

**Translation**

Resting the mind steadfastly on its Goal (Brahman) after detaching itself from manifold sense objects by continually observing their defects,
