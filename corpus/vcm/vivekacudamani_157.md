## Text 157

**Sanskrit**

देहतद्धमर्तत्कमर्तदवस्थािदसािक्षणः ।
सत एव स्वतःिसद्धं तद्वैलक्षण्यमात्मनः ॥ १५७ ॥

**Transliteration**

dehataddharmatatkarmatadavasthādisākṣiṇaḥ |
sata eva svataḥsiddhaṃ tadvailakṣaṇyamātmanaḥ || 157 ||

**Translation**

“It is self-evident that the Self is the enduring Reality, that It is different from the body, its characteristics, states and activities, and that It is the Witness of them all.”
