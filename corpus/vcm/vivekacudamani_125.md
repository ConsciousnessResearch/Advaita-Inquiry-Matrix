## Text 125

**Sanskrit**

यो िवजानाित सकलं जाग्रत्स्वप्नसुषुिप्तषु ।
बुिद्धतद्वृित्तसद्भावमभावमहिमत्ययम् ॥ १२६ ॥

**Transliteration**

asti kaścitsvayaṃ nityamahaṃpratyayalambanaḥ |
avasthātrayasākṣī saṃpañcakośavilakṣaṇaḥ || 125 ||

**Translation**

“There is something which exists by itself as the substratum of the consciousness of ‘I’. Being the witness of the three states, it is different from the ﬁve sheaths. That is the Ātman.”
