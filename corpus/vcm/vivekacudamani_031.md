## Text 31

**Sanskrit**

मोक्षकारणसामग्र्यां भिक्तरेव गरीयसी ।
स्वस्वरूपानुसन्धानं भिक्तिरत्यिभधीयते ॥ ३१ ॥

**Transliteration**

mokṣakāraṇasāmagryāṃ bhaktireva garīyasī |
svasvarūpānusandhānaṃ bhaktirityabhidhīyate || 31 ||

**Translation**

Among qualities conducive to Liberation, bhakti (devotion) holds the supreme place. Devotion is deﬁned as seeking one’s real nature.
