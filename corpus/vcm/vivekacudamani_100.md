## Text 100

**Sanskrit**

सवर्व्यापृितकरणं िलङ्गिमदं स्यािच्चदात्मनः पु
ंसः ।
वास्यािदकिमव तक्ष्णस्तेनैवात्मा भवत्यसङ्गोऽयम् ॥ १०० ॥

**Transliteration**

sarvavyāpṛtikaraṇaṃ liṅgamidaṃ syāccidātmanaḥ puṃsaḥ |
vāsyādikamiva takṣṇastenaivātmā bhavatyasaṅgo'yam || 100 ||

**Translation**

This liṅga-śarīra (subtle body) is the indispensable operative cause of all the activities of the individual self, which is to the nature of intelligence as tools are to a carpenter. For that reason the Ātman is unattached.
