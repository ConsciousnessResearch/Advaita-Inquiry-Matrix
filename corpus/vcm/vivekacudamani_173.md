## Text 173

**Sanskrit**

देहािदसवर्िवषये पिरकल्प्य रागं
बध्नाित तेन पुरुषं पशुवद्गुणेन ।
वैरस्यमत्र िवषवत्सुवुधाय पश्चाद्
एनं िवमोचयित तन्मन एव बन्धात् ॥ १७३ ॥

**Transliteration**

dehādisarvaviṣaye parikalpya rāgaṃ
badhnāti tena puruṣaṃ paśuvadguṇena |
vairasyamatra viṣavatsuvudhāya paścād
enaṃ vimocayati tanmana eva bandhāt || 173 ||

**Translation**

“The mind produces attachment to objects like the body etc., binding a man like an animal bound with a rope. Later, the same mind creates aversion for them like poison, and liberates him from that self-same bondage.“
