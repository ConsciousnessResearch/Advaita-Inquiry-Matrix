## Text 66

**Sanskrit**

तस्मात्सवर्प्रयत्नेन भवबन्धिवमुक्तये ।
स्वैरेव यत्नः कतर्व्यो रोगादािवव पिण्डतैः ॥ ६६ ॥

**Transliteration**

tasmātsarvaprayatnena bhavabandhavimuktaye |
svaireva yatnaḥ kartavyo rogādāviva paṇḍitaiḥ || 66 ||

**Translation**

“Therefore the wise seeker should, as in the case of illness etc., strive by every means at his disposal to be free from the bondage of repeated births and deaths.”
