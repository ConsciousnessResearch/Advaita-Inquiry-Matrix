## Text 155

**Sanskrit**

पूवर्ं जनेरिधमृतेरिप नायमिस्त
जातक्षणः क्षणगुणोऽिनयतस्वभावः ।
नैको जडश्च घटवत्पिरदृश्यमानः
स्वात्मा कथं भवित भाविवकारवेत्ता ॥ १५५ ॥

**Transliteration**

pūrvaṃ janeradhimṛterapi nāyamasti
jātakṣaṇaḥ kṣaṇaguṇo'niyatasvabhāvaḥ |
naiko jaḍaśca ghaṭavatparidṛśyamānaḥ
svātmā kathaṃ bhavati bhāvavikāravettā || 155 ||

**Translation**

“It does not exist before its origination or after its destruction. Every moment it is subject to birth and death, and so is impermanent—it is not of the same nature always. It is material and visible like a pot. How can it be the Ātman, which knows the changes of existence?“
