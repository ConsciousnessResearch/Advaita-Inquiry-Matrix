## Text 56

**Sanskrit**

न योगेन न सांख्येन कमर्णा नो न िवद्यया ।
ब्रह्मात्मैकत्वबोधेन मोक्षः िसध्यित नान्यथा ॥ ५६ ॥

**Transliteration**

na yogena na sāṃkhyena karmaṇā no na vidyayā |
brahmātmaikatvabodhena mokṣaḥ sidhyati nānyathā || 56 ||

**Translation**

“Neither by yoga, nor by Sāṅkhya, nor by action, nor by learning, is liberation possible. Only by the realization of the oneness of Brahman, the Absolute, and Ātman, the Self, is liberation possible, and in no other way.”
