## Text 17

**Sanskrit**

िववेिकनो िवरक्तस्य शमािदगुणशािलनः ।
मुमुक्षोरेव िह ब्रह्मिजज्ञासायोग्यता मता ॥ १७ ॥

**Transliteration**

vivekino viraktasya śamādiguṇaśālinaḥ |
mumukṣoreva hi brahmajijñāsāyogyatā matā || 17 ||

**Translation**

A man who can discriminate between the Real and the unreal, whose mind is turned away from the unreal, who possesses the six virtues of calmness etc., and who is longing for Liberation, is alone considered qualiﬁed to inquire into Brahman.
