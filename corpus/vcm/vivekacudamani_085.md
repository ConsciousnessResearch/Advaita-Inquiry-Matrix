## Text 85

**Sanskrit**

मोह एव महामृत्युमु
र्मुक्षोवर्पुरािदषु ।
मोहो िविनिजर् तो येन स मुिक्तपदमहर्ित ॥ ८५ ॥

**Transliteration**

moha eva mahāmṛtyurmumukṣorvapurādiṣu |
moho vinirjito yena sa muktipadamarhati || 85 ||

**Translation**

“For the seeker of liberation, attachment to the body is a tragic death. He alone who is free from this attachment deserves liberation.”
