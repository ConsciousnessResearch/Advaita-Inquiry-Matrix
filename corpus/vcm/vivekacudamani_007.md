## Text 7

**Sanskrit**

अमृतत्वस्य नाशािस्त िवत्तेनेत्येव िह श्रुितः ।
ब्रवीित कमर्णो मुक्तेरहेतुत्वं स्फु टं यतः ॥ ७ ॥

**Transliteration**

amṛtatvasya nāśāsti vittenetyeva hi śrutiḥ |
bravīti karmaṇo mukterahetutvaṃ sphuṭaṃ yataḥ || 7 ||

**Translation**

There is no hope of immortality by means of riches— such indeed is the declaration of the Vedas. Hence it is clear: works cannot be the cause of Liberation.
