## Text 84

**Sanskrit**

शरीरपोषणाथीर् सन् य आत्मानं िददृक्षित ।
ग्राहं दारुिधया धृत्वा निद ततु
र्ं स गच्छित ॥ ८४ ॥

**Transliteration**

śarīrapoṣaṇārthī san ya ātmānaṃ didṛkṣati |
grāhaṃ dārudhiyā dhṛtvā nadi tartuṃ sa gacchati || 84 ||

**Translation**

“He who wishes to realize Ātman through indulgence of the body is like one who wishes to cross a river on the back of a crocodile, thinking it is a wooden log.”
