## Text 87

**Sanskrit**

त्वङ् मांसरुिधरस्नायुमेदोमज्जािस्थसंकु लम् ।
पूणर्ं मूत्रपुरीषाभ्यां स्थूलं िनन्द्यिमदं वपुः ॥ ८७ ॥

**Transliteration**

tvaṅmāṃsarudhirasnāyumedomajjāsthisaṃkulam |
pūrṇaṃ mūtrapurīṣābhyāṃ sthūlaṃ nindyamidaṃ vapuḥ || 87 ||

**Translation**

This gross body is despicable as it consists of skin, ﬂesh, blood, blood-vessels, fat, marrow and bones, urine and feces.
