## Text 72

**Sanskrit**

मज्जािस्थमेदःपलरक्तचमर्
त्वगाह्वयैधार्तुिभरेिभरिन्वतम् ।
पादोरुवक्षोभुजपृष्ठमस्तकै ः
अङ्गैरुपाङ्गैरुपयुक्तमेतत् ॥ ७२ ॥

**Transliteration**

majjāsthimedaḥpalaraktacarma
tvagāhvayairdhātubhirebhiranvitam |
pādoruvakṣobhujapṛṣṭhamastakaiḥ
aṅgairupāṅgairupayuktametat || 72 ||

**Translation**

“Composed of the seven ingredients: marrow, bones, fat, ﬂesh, blood, dermis and epidermis, and consisting of the following parts: legs, thighs, chest, arms, back and the head…“
