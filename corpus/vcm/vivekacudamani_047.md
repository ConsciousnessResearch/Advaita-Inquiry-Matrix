## Text 47

**Sanskrit**

अज्ञानयोगात्परमात्मनस्तव
ह्यनात्मबन्धस्तत एव संसृितः ।
तयोिवर् वेकोिदतबोधविन्हः
अज्ञानकायर्ं प्रदहेत्समूलम् ॥ ४७ ॥

**Transliteration**

ajñānayogātparamātmanastava
hyanātmabandhastata eva saṃsṛtiḥ |
tayorvivekoditabodhavanhiḥ
ajñānakāryaṃ pradahetsamūlam || 47 ||

**Translation**

“You are indeed the Supreme Self; but due to association with ignorance, you ﬁnd yourself in bondage to the not-Self, which is the sole cause of the cycle of births and deaths. All the effects of ignorance—root and branch—are burnt by the ﬁre of knowledge, which arises from discrimination between Self and the not-Self.”
