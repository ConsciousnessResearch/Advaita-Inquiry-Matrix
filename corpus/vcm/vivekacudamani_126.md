## Text 126

**Sanskrit**

यो िवजानाित सकलं जाग्रत्स्वप्नसुषुिप्तषु ।
बुिद्धतद्वृित्तसद्भावमभावमहिमत्ययम् ॥ १२६ ॥

**Transliteration**

yo vijānāti sakalaṃ jāgratsvapnasuṣuptiṣu |
buddhitadvṛttisadbhāvamabhāvamahamityayam || 126 ||

**Translation**

“That which as ‘I’ knows everything in the waking, dream and dreamless sleep states, the presence of the buddhi and its actions, and their absence—that is the Ātman.
