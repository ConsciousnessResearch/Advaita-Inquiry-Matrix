## Text 172

**Sanskrit**

वायुनानीयते मेघ: पुनस्तेनैव नीयते ।
मनसा कल्प्यते बन्धो मोक्षस्तेनैव कल्प्यते ॥ १७२ ॥

**Transliteration**

vāyunānīyate meghaḥ punastenaiva nīyate |
manasā kalpyate bandho mokṣastenaiva kalpyate || 172 ||

**Translation**

“The clouds are brought together by the wind, and they are again dispersed by it. Bondage is created by the mind, and liberation is also brought about by it.”
