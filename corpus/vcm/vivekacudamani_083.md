## Text 83

**Sanskrit**

शरीरपोषणाथीर् सन् य आत्मानं िददृक्षित ।
ग्राहं दारुिधया धृत्वा निद ततु
र्ं स गच्छित ॥ ८४ ॥
मोह एव महामृत्युमु
मोहो िविनिजर् तो येन स मुिक्तपदमहर्ित ॥ ८५ ॥
र्मुक्षोवर्पुरािदषु ।

**Transliteration**

anukṣaṇaṃ yatparihṛtya kṛtyaṃ
anādyavidyākṛtabandhamokṣaṇam |
dehaḥ parārtho'yamamuṣya poṣaṇe
yaḥ sajjate sa svamanena hanti || 83 ||

**Translation**

“This body is essentially an instrument for realizing Paramātman. He who gives up what should continuously be done—namely, getting release from the bondage wrought by beginningless ignorance— and indulges in gratifying the body, which is ever for others, slays himself.“
