## Text 186

**Sanskrit**

भुङ् क्ते िविचत्रास्विप योिनषु व्रजन्
नायाित िनयार्त्यध ऊध्वर्मेषः ।
अस्यैव िवज्ञानमायास्य जाग्रत्
स्वप्नाद्यवस्थाः सुखदुःखभोगः ॥ १८७ ॥

**Transliteration**

anādikālo'yamahaṃsvabhāvo
jīvaḥ samastavyavahāravoḍhā |
karoti karmāṇyapi pūrvavāsanaḥ
puṇyānyapuṇyāni ca tatphalāni || 186 ||

**Translation**

“It is without beginning, is of the nature of the ego (I- sense), and is called the embodied self, which carries out the entire range of activities on the relative plane. It performs good and evil actions according to its previous latent tendencies, and experiences their results.“
