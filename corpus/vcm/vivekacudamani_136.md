## Text 136

**Sanskrit**

ं त्वं स्वमात्मानमात्मन्य्
िनयिमतमनसामु
अयमहिमित साक्षािद्विद्ध बुिद्धप्रसादात् ।
ं
जिनमरणतरङ्गापारसंसारिसन्धु
प्रतर भव कृ ताथोर् ब्रह्मरूपेण संस्थः ॥ १३६ ॥

**Transliteration**

niyamitamanasāmuṃ tvaṃ svamātmānamātmany
ayamahamiti sākṣādviddhi buddhiprasādāt |
janimaraṇataraṅgāpārasaṃsārasindhuṃ
pratara bhava kṛtārtho brahmarūpeṇa saṃsthaḥ || 136 ||

**Translation**

“Directly realise Ātman as your ‘I’ by means of a controlled mind and puriﬁed intellect. Cross this shoeless ocean of saṃsāra, with its waves of birth and death, by identifying yourself with the Self. Thus established as Brahman, be a person of fulﬁlled purpose.“
