## Text 146

**Sanskrit**

अज्ञानमूलोऽयमनात्मबन्धो
नैसिगर् कोऽनािदरनन्त ईिरतः ।
जन्माप्ययव्यािधजरािददुःख
प्रवाहपातं जनयत्यमुष्य ॥ १४६ ॥

**Transliteration**

ajñānamūlo'yamanātmabandho
naisargiko'nādirananta īritaḥ |
janmāpyayavyādhijarādiduḥkha
pravāhapātaṃ janayatyamuṣya || 146 ||

**Translation**

“This bondage caused by the non-Self is rooted in ignorance and is self-caused. It is described as beginningless and endless. It subjects one to an endless ﬂood of miseries: birth, death, disease and old age.“ Ātma and Anatma
