## Text 181

**Sanskrit**

तन्मनःशोधनं कायर्ं प्रयत्नेन मुमुक्षुणा ।
िवशुद्धे सित चैतिस्मन्मुिक्तः करफलायते ॥ १८१ ॥

**Transliteration**

tanmanaḥśodhanaṃ kāryaṃ prayatnena mumukṣuṇā |
viśuddhe sati caitasminmuktiḥ karaphalāyate || 181 ||

**Translation**

“Therefore, the man who earnestly desires liberation must diligently purify his mind. For one whose mind is completely puriﬁed, liberation is as easily realised as a fruit in the palm of one’s hand.”
