## Text 86

**Sanskrit**

मोहं जिह महामृत्यु
ं देहदारसुतािदषु ।
यं िजत्वा मुनयो यािन्त तिद्वष्णोः परमं पदम् ॥ ८६ ॥

**Transliteration**

mohaṃ jahi mahāmṛtyuṃ dehadārasutādiṣu |
yaṃ jitvā munayo yānti tadviṣṇoḥ paramaṃ padam || 86 ||

**Translation**

Give up your attachment to the body, wife and sons, which is the great death. Conquering it, the sages
