## Text 40

**Sanskrit**

कथं तरेयं भविसन्धुमेतं
का वा गितमेर् कतमोऽस्त्युपायः ।
जाने न िकिञ्ज्चत्कृ पयाव मां प्रभो
संसारदुःखक्षितमातनुष्व ॥ ४० ॥

**Transliteration**

kathaṃ tareyaṃ bhavasindhumetaṃ
kā vā gatirme katamo'styupāyaḥ |
jāne na kiñjcitkṛpayāva māṃ prabho
saṃsāraduḥkhakṣatimātanuṣva || 40 ||

**Translation**

“How to cross this ocean of worldly existence? What is to be my ultimate destination? Which of the many means should I adopt? I know nothing of these. O Lord! Save me and describe in all detail how to end the misery of this earthly existence.”
