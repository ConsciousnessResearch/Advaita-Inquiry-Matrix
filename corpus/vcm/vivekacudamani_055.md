## Text 55

**Sanskrit**

अिवद्याकामकमार्िदपाशबन्धं िवमोिचतुम् ।
कः शक्नुयािद्वनात्मानं कल्पकोिटशतैरिप ॥ ५५ ॥

**Transliteration**

avidyākāmakarmādipāśabandhaṃ vimocitum |
kaḥ śaknuyādvinātmānaṃ kalpakoṭiśatairapi || 55 ||

**Translation**

“Who else but oneself can help rid oneself of the bondage caused by the chains of ignorance, desire, action, etc.—even in hundreds of millions of years?”
