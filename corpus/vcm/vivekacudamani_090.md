## Text 90

**Sanskrit**

सवार्िप बाह्यसंसारः पुरुषस्य यदाश्रयः ।
िविद्ध देहिमदं स्थूलं गृहवद्गृहमेिधनः ॥ ९0 ॥

**Transliteration**

sarvāpi bāhyasaṃsāraḥ puruṣasya yadāśrayaḥ |
viddhi dehamidaṃ sthūlaṃ gṛhavadgṛhamedhinaḥ || 90 ||

**Translation**

“Know that this gross body is that on which rests man’s entire contact with the external world, and is like the house of a materialistic householder.”
