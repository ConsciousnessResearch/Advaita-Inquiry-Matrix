## Text 43

**Sanskrit**

मा भैष्ट िवद्वंस्तव नास्त्यपायः
संसारिसन्धोस्तरणेऽस्त्युपायः ।
येनैव याता यतयोऽस्य पारं
तमेव मागर्ं तव िनिदर् शािम ॥ ४३ ॥

**Transliteration**

mā bhaiṣṭa vidvaṃstava nāstyapāyaḥ
saṃsārasindhostaraṇe'styupāyaḥ |
yenaiva yātā yatayo'sya pāraṃ
tameva mārgaṃ tava nirdiśāmi || 43 ||

**Translation**

“Fear not, O learned one; there is no danger for thee; there is a means of crossing this sea of relative existence; that very way by which sages have gone beyond it, I shall inculcate to thee.”
