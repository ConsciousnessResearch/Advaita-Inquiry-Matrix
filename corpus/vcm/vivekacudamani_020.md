## Text 20

**Sanskrit**

ब्रह्म सत्यं जगिन्मथ्येत्येवंरूपो िविनश्चयः ।
सोऽयं िनत्यािनत्यवस्तुिववेकः समुदाहृतः ॥ २० ॥

**Transliteration**

brahma satyaṃ jaganmithyetyevaṃrūpo viniścayaḥ |
so'yaṃ nityānityavastuvivekaḥ samudāhṛtaḥ || 20 ||

**Translation**

A ﬁrm conviction that Brahman alone is Real and the universe unreal is known as viveka: discrimination between the Real and the unreal.
