## Text 174

**Sanskrit**

िववेकवैराग्यगुणाितरेकाच्
छु द्धत्वमासाद्य मनो िवमुक्त्यै ।
भवत्यतो बुिद्धमतो मुमुक्षोस्
ताभ्यां दृढाभ्यां भिवतव्यमग्रे ॥ १७५ ॥

**Transliteration**

tasmānmanaḥ kāraṇamasya jantoḥ
bandhasya mokṣasya ca vā vidhāne |
bandhasya heturmalinaṃ rajoguṇaiḥ
mokṣasya śuddhaṃ virajastamaskam || 174 ||

**Translation**

“Therefore, the mind is the cause of both liberation and bondage. When tainted by the effects of rajas, it causes bondage. When it is free from rajas and tamas, it becomes the cause of liberation.“
