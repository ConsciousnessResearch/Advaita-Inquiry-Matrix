## Text 80

**Sanskrit**

िवषयाख्यग्रहो येन सुिवरक्त्यिसना हतः ।
स गच्छित भवाम्भोधेः पारं प्रत्यूहविजर् तः ॥ ८० ॥

**Transliteration**

viṣayākhyagraho yena suviraktyasinā hataḥ |
sa gacchati bhavāmbhodheḥ pāraṃ pratyūhavarjitaḥ || 80 ||

**Translation**

“He who slays the crocodile called ‘sense-objects’ with the sword of mature dispassion
