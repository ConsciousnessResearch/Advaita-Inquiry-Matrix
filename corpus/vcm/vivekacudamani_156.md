## Text 156

**Sanskrit**

पािणपादािदमान्देहो नात्मा व्यङ्गेऽिप जीवनात् ।
तत्तच्छक्तेरनाशाच्च न िनयम्यो िनयामकः ॥ १५६ ॥

**Transliteration**

pāṇipādādimāndeho nātmā vyaṅge'pi jīvanāt |
tattacchakteranāśācca na niyamyo niyāmakaḥ || 156 ||

**Translation**

“The body has hands, feet etc. It is not the Ātman because one is able to live even when limbs are amputated, as their powers are not lost. The ruled cannot be the ruler.”
