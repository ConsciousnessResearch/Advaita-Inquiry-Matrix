## Text 6

**Sanskrit**

वदन्तु शास्त्रािण यजन्तु देवान्
कु वर्न्तु कमार्िण भजन्तु देवताः ।
आत्मैक्यबोधेन िवनािप मुिक्तः
न िसध्यित ब्रह्मशतान्तरेऽिप ॥ ६ ॥

**Transliteration**

vadantu śāstrāṇi yajantu devān
kurvantu karmāṇi bhajantu devatāḥ |
ātmaikyabodhena vināpi muktiḥ
na sidhyati brahmaśatāntare'pi || 6 ||

**Translation**

Let people quote scriptures and sacriﬁce to the gods; let them perform rituals and worship the deities; but there is no Liberation without realization of one’s identity with Ātman—
