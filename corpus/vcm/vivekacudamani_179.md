## Text 179

**Sanskrit**

अध्यासदोषात्पुरुषस्य संसृितः
अध्यासबन्धस्त्वमुनैव किल्पतः ।
रजस्तमोदोषवतोऽिववेिकनो
जन्मािददुःखस्य िनदानमेतत् ॥ १७९ ॥

**Transliteration**

adhyāsadoṣātpuruṣasya saṃsṛtiḥ
adhyāsabandhastvamunaiva kalpitaḥ |
rajastamodoṣavato'vivekino
janmādiduḥkhasya nidānametat || 179 ||

**Translation**

“The defect of superimposition causes transmigration. The bondage of superimposition is created by the mind alone. It is the primal cause of the griefs of birth, suffering etc. for the man without discrimination, caught up in rajas and tamas.“
