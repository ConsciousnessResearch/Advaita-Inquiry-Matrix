## Text 78

**Sanskrit**

िवषयाशामहापाशाद्यो िवमुक्तः सुदुस्त्यजात् ।
स एव कल्पते मुक्त्यै नान्यः षट्शास्त्रवेद्यिप ॥ ७८ ॥

**Transliteration**

viṣayāśāmahāpāśādyo vimuktaḥ sudustyajāt |
sa eva kalpate muktyai nānyaḥ ṣaṭśāstravedyapi || 78 ||

**Translation**

“One who is liberated from the terrible bonds of desires for sense objects, so very difﬁcult to renounce, is alone ﬁt for liberation—and no one else, even if well-versed in all six schools of Vedic philosophy.”
