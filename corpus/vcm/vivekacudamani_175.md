## Text 175

**Sanskrit**

िववेकवैराग्यगुणाितरेकाच्
छु द्धत्वमासाद्य मनो िवमुक्त्यै ।
भवत्यतो बुिद्धमतो मुमुक्षोस्
ताभ्यां दृढाभ्यां भिवतव्यमग्रे ॥ १७५ ॥

**Transliteration**

vivekavairāgyaguṇātirekāc
chuddhatvamāsādya mano vimuktyai |
bhavatyato buddhimato mumukṣos
tābhyāṃ dṛḍhābhyāṃ bhavitavyamagre || 175 ||

**Translation**

“By attaining purity through cultivation of ﬁrm discrimination and uncompromising renunciation, the mind gets qualiﬁed for liberaton. So, a seeker for liberation must be ﬁrmly established in them ﬁrst.“
