## Text 137

**Sanskrit**

ंसः
अत्रानात्मन्यहिमित मितबर्न्ध एषोऽस्य पु
प्राप्तोऽज्ञानाज्जननमरणक्लेशसंपातहेतुः ।
येनैवायं वपुिरदमसत्सत्यिमत्यात्मबुद्ध्या
पुष्यत्युक्षत्यवित िवषयैस्तन्तुिभः कोशकृ द्वत् ॥ १३७ ॥

**Transliteration**

atrānātmanyahamiti matirbandha eṣo'sya puṃsaḥ
prāpto'jñānājjananamaraṇakleśasaṃpātahetuḥ |
yenaivāyaṃ vapuridamasatsatyamityātmabuddhyā
puṣyatyukṣatyavati viṣayaistantubhiḥ kośakṛdvat || 137 ||

**Translation**

“The belief that the anātman is the Ātman is man’s bondage. This belief arises from avidyā. It is the cause of griefs like birth, death etc. Thinking this unreal body to be real like the Ātman nourishes it, bathes it, protects it by sense objects, and gets bound by them, like the silkworm by its threads.“
