## Text 49

**Sanskrit**

को नाम बन्धः कथमेष आगतः
कथं प्रितष्ठास्य कथं िवमोक्षः ।
कोऽसावनात्मा परमः क आत्मा
तयोिवर् वेकः कथमेतदुच्यताम् ॥ ४९ ॥

**Transliteration**

ko nāma bandhaḥ kathameṣa āgataḥ
kathaṃ pratiṣṭhāsya kathaṃ vimokṣaḥ |
ko'sāvanātmā paramaḥ ka ātmā
tayorvivekaḥ kathametaducyatām || 49 ||

**Translation**

“What, indeed, is bondage? How has it come? How does it continue to exist? How can one get out of it completely? What is this non-Self? Who is the Supreme Self? What is the process of discrimination between them? Please explain all these to me.“
