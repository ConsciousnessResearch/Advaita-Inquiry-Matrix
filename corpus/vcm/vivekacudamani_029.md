## Text 29

**Sanskrit**

वैराग्यं च मुमुक्षुत्वं तीव्रं यस्य तु िवद्यते ।
तिस्मन्नेवाथर्वन्तः स्युः फलवन्तः शमादयः ॥ २९ ॥

**Transliteration**

vairāgyaṃ ca mumukṣutvaṃ tīvraṃ yasya tu vidyate |
tasminnevārthavantaḥ syuḥ phalavantaḥ śamādayaḥ || 29 ||

**Translation**

Calmness and other practices have their meaning and bear fruit indeed only in one who has an intense spirit of renunciation and yearning for liberation.
