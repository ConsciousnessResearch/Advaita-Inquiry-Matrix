## Text 9

**Sanskrit**

उद्धरेदात्मनात्मानं मग्नं संसारवािरधौ ।
योगारूढत्वमासाद्य सम्यग्दशर्निनष्ठया ॥ ९ ॥

**Transliteration**

uddharedātmanātmānaṃ magnaṃ saṃsāravāridhau |
yogārūḍhatvamāsādya samyagdarśananiṣṭhayā || 9 ||

**Translation**

Becoming absorbed in profound abstract meditation through devotion to right discrimination, one should lift oneself from the ocean of birth and death wherein one has come to be drowned.
