## Text 57

**Sanskrit**

वीणाया रूपसौन्दयर्ं तन्त्रीवादनसौष्ठवम् ।
प्रजारञ्ज्जनमात्रं तन्न साम्राज्याय कल्पते ॥ ५७ ॥

**Transliteration**

vīṇāyā rūpasaundaryaṃ tantrīvādanasauṣṭhavam |
prajārañjjanamātraṃ tanna sāmrājyāya kalpate || 57 ||

**Translation**

“The beautiful form of the vīnā and the proﬁciency of one playing its melodies serve but to please an audience; they do not, by themselves, ever prove sufﬁcient to confer full sovereignty.”
