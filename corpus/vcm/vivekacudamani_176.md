## Text 176

**Sanskrit**

मनो नाम महाव्याघ्रो िवषयारण्यभूिमषु ।
चरत्यत्र न गच्छन्तु साधवो ये मुमुक्षवः ॥ १७६ ॥

**Transliteration**

mano nāma mahāvyāghro viṣayāraṇyabhūmiṣu |
caratyatra na gacchantu sādhavo ye mumukṣavaḥ || 176 ||

**Translation**

“A terrible tiger called the mind wanders in the vast forest of sense objects. Let the good persons who seek salvation not go there.”
