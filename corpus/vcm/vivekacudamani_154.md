## Text 154

**Sanskrit**

देहोऽयमन्नभवनोऽन्नमायास्तु कोशः
चान्नेन जीवित िवनश्यित तिद्वहीनः ।
त्वक्चमर्मांसरुिधरािस्थपुरीषरािशः
नायं स्वयं भिवतुमहर्ित िनत्यशुद्धः ॥ १५४ ॥

**Transliteration**

deho'yamannabhavano'nnamāyāstu kośaḥ
cānnena jīvati vinaśyati tadvihīnaḥ |
tvakcarmamāṃsarudhirāsthipurīṣarāśiḥ
nāyaṃ svayaṃ bhavitumarhati nityaśuddhaḥ || 154 ||

**Translation**

“The sheath of the gross body is annamaya: food-born and food-sustained. It lives by food and dies without it. It is an aggregate of skin, ﬂesh, blood and the excreta. It does not deserve to be the ever-pure Ātman.“
