## Text 11

**Sanskrit**

िचत्तस्य शुद्धये कमर् न तु वस्तूपलब्धये ।
वस्तुिसिद्धिवर् चारेण न िकं िचत्कमर्कोिटिभः ॥ ११ ॥

**Transliteration**

cittasya śuddhaye karma na tu vastūpalabdhaye |
vastusiddhirvicāreṇa na kiṃcitkarmakoṭibhiḥ || 11 ||

**Translation**

Karma-yoga leads to puriﬁcation of the mind, not to perception of the Reality. Realization of Truth is attained by discrimination, and not in the least by ten million actions.
