## Text 183

**Sanskrit**

मनोमयो नािप भवेत्परात्मा
ह्याद्यन्तवत्त्वात्पिरणािमभावात् ।
दुःखात्मकत्वािद्वषयत्वहेतोः
द्रष्टा िह दृश्यात्मतया न दृष्टः ॥ १८३ ॥

**Transliteration**

manomayo nāpi bhavetparātmā
hyādyantavattvātpariṇāmibhāvāt |
duḥkhātmakatvādviṣayatvahetoḥ
draṣṭā hi dṛśyātmatayā na dṛṣṭaḥ || 183 ||

**Translation**

“The manomaya-kośa also cannot be the Paramātma, for it has a beginning and an end, is subject to modiﬁcations, is of the nature of suffering, and is an object. The seen object cannot be the seeing subject.“
