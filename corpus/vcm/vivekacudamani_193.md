## Text 193

**Sanskrit**

अतोऽस्य जीवभावोऽिप िनत्या भवित संसृितः ।
न िनवतेर्त तन्मोक्षः कथं मे श्रीगुरो वद ॥ १९३ ॥

**Transliteration**

ato'sya jīvabhāvo'pi nityā bhavati saṃsṛtiḥ |
na nivarteta tanmokṣaḥ kathaṃ me śrīguro vada || 193 ||

**Translation**

“So the embodied state of the Self must also be without end, ever subject to transmigration. Please tell me, O revered Master, how then there can be liberation for the Self?“
