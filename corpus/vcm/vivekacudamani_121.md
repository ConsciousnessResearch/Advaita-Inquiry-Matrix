## Text 121

**Sanskrit**

सवर्प्रकारप्रिमितप्रशािन्तः
बीजात्मनाविस्थितरेव बुद्धेः ।
सुषुिप्तरेतस्य िकल प्रतीितः
िकं िचन्न वेद्मीित जगत्प्रिसद्धेः ॥ १२१ ॥

**Transliteration**

sarvaprakārapramitipraśāntiḥ
bījātmanāvasthitireva buddheḥ |
suṣuptiretasya kila pratītiḥ
kiṃcinna vedmīti jagatprasiddheḥ || 121 ||

**Translation**

“In suṣupti all pramaṇas (sources of knowledge) are still. The buddhi remains only in the form of a seed. The test of this is the universal verdict: ‘I did not know anything while asleep’.“
