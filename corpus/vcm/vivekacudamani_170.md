## Text 170

**Sanskrit**

स्वप्नेऽथर्शून्ये सृजित स्वशक्त्या
भोक्त्रािदिवश्वं मन एव सवर्म् ।
तथैव जाग्रत्यिप नो िवशेषः
तत्सवर्मेतन्मनसो िवजृम्भणम् ॥ १७० ॥

**Transliteration**

svapne'rthaśūnye sṛjati svaśaktyā
bhoktrādiviśvaṃ mana eva sarvam |
tathaiva jāgratyapi no viśeṣaḥ
tatsarvametanmanaso vijṛmbhaṇam || 170 ||

**Translation**

“In the absence of external objects in dream, the mind alone creates everything—the enjoyer etc.—by its power. So, too, there is no difference in the waking state. All that is also only a projection of the mind.“
