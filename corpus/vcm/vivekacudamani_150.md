## Text 150

**Sanskrit**

तच्छैवालापनये सम्यक्सिललं प्रतीयते शुद्धम् ।
ंसः ॥ १५० ॥
तृष्णासन्तापहरं सद्यः सौख्यप्रदं परं पु

**Transliteration**

tacchaivālāpanaye samyaksalilaṃ pratīyate śuddham |
tṛṣṇāsantāpaharaṃ sadyaḥ saukhyapradaṃ paraṃ puṃsaḥ || 150 ||

**Translation**

“When the moss is removed, the water becomes visible in all clarity. It removes the pangs of thirst and immediately ﬁlls men with happiness.“
