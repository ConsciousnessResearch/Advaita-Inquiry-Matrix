## Text 27

**Sanskrit**

अहंकारािददेहान्तान् बन्धानज्ञानकिल्पतान् ।
स्वस्वरूपावबोधेन मोक्तुिमच्छा मुमुक्षुता ॥ २७ ॥

**Transliteration**

ahaṃkārādidehāntān bandhānajñānakalpitān |
svasvarūpāvabodhena moktumicchā mumukṣutā || 27 ||

**Translation**

Mumukṣutvām or yearning for liberation is the desire to free oneself from all bondage superimposed by ignorance—from egoism to the body— by realizing one’s true nature.
