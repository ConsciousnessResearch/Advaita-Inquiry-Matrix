## Text 94

**Sanskrit**

अत्रािभमानादहिमत्यहंकृ ितः ।
स्वाथार्नुसन्धानगुणेन िचत्तम् ॥ ९४ ॥

**Transliteration**

atrābhimānādahamityahaṃkṛtiḥ |
svārthānusandhānaguṇena cittam || 94 ||

**Translation**

“The ego is so called by reason of its identiﬁcation with the body as one‘s own self, and citta from its function of constantly emembering and contemplating things of its interest.
