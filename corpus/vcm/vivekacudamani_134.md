## Text 134

**Sanskrit**

प्रकृ ितिवकृ ितिभन्नः शुद्धबोधस्वभावः
सदसिददमशेषं भासयिन्निवर् शेषः ।
िवलसित परमात्मा जाग्रदािदष्ववस्था
स्वहमहिमित साक्षात्सािक्षरूपेण बुद्धेः ॥ १३५ ॥

**Transliteration**

na jāyate no mriyate na vardhate
na kṣīyate no vikaroti nityaḥ |
vilīyamāne'pi vapuṣyamuṣmin
na līyate kumbha ivāmbaraṃ svayam || 134 ||

**Translation**

“The Ātman is not born; it does not die; it does not grow or decline; it does not change. It is eternal. Even if this body is destroyed, it does not become extinct—even as space does not become extinct on the destruction of the pot which enclosed it.“
