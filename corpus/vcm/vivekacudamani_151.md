## Text 151

**Sanskrit**

पञ्चानामिप कोशानामपवादे िवभात्ययं शुद्धः ।
िनत्यानन्दैकरसः प्रत्यग्रूपः परः स्वयंज्योितः ॥ १५१ ॥

**Transliteration**

pañcānāmapi kośānāmapavāde vibhātyayaṃ śuddhaḥ |
nityānandaikarasaḥ pratyagrūpaḥ paraḥ svayaṃjyotiḥ || 151 ||

**Translation**

“When the ﬁve sheaths are set aside, this pure, ever- blissful, indwelling, Supreme Self-effulgent Ātman appears clear—untouched by the anatman.“
