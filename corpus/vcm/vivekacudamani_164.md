## Text 164

**Sanskrit**

देहात्मधीरेव नृणामसिद्धयां
जन्मािददुःखप्रभवस्य बीजम् ।
यतस्ततस्त्वं जिह तां प्रयत्नात्
त्यक्ते तु िचत्ते न पुनभर्वाशा ॥ १६४ ॥

**Transliteration**

dehātmadhīreva nṛṇāmasaddhiyāṃ
janmādiduḥkhaprabhavasya bījam |
yatastatastvaṃ jahi tāṃ prayatnāt
tyakte tu citte na punarbhavāśā || 164 ||

**Translation**

“For those whose intellect rests on asat, the notion that the body is the Ātman causes the arising of sorrows, beginning with birth. Therefore, put an end to this notion with all effort. Once the mind is detached from asat, there is no possibility of being born again.“
