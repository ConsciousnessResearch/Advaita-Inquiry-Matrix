## Text 89

**Sanskrit**

बाह्येिन्द्रयैः स्थूलपदाथर्सेवां
स्रक्चन्दनस्त्र्यािदिविचत्ररूपाम् ।
करोित जीवः स्वयमेतदात्मना
तस्मात्प्रशिस्तवर्पुषोऽस्य जागरे ॥ ८९ ॥

**Transliteration**

bāhyendriyaiḥ sthūlapadārthasevāṃ
srakcandanastryādivicitrarūpām |
karoti jīvaḥ svayametadātmanā
tasmātpraśastirvapuṣo'sya jāgare || 89 ||

**Translation**

“Identifying itself with the body, the jīva enjoys gross objects like garlands, sandalwood paste, women etc., through the external organs. Hence the importance of this body in waking consciousness.“
