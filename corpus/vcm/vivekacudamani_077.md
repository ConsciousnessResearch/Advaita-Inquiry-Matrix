## Text 77

**Sanskrit**

दोषेण तीव्रो िवषयः कृ ष्णसपर्िवषादिप ।
िवषं िनहिन्त भोक्तारं द्रष्टारं चक्षुषाप्ययम् ॥ ७७ ॥

**Transliteration**

doṣeṇa tīvro viṣayaḥ kṛṣṇasarpaviṣādapi |
viṣaṃ nihanti bhoktāraṃ draṣṭāraṃ cakṣuṣāpyayam || 77 ||

**Translation**

“Sense objects are even more virulent than a king cobra. Poison is fatal to one who swallows it, but the sense objects kill him who merely looks at them with his eyes.”
