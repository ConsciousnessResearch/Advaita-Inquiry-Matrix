## Text 64

**Sanskrit**

अकृ त्वा शत्रुसंहारमगत्वािखलभूिश्रयम् ।
राजाहिमित शब्दान्नो राजा भिवतुमहर्ित ॥ ६४ ॥

**Transliteration**

akṛtvā śatrusaṃhāramagatvākhilabhūśriyam |
rājāhamiti śabdānno rājā bhavitumarhati || 64 ||

**Translation**

“Without eliminating his enemies and bringing the whole wealth of the kingdom under his sway, one cannot become an emperor by merely repeating, ‘I am the Emperor’.”
