## Text 187

**Sanskrit**

देहािदिनष्ठाश्रमधमर्कमर् गुणािभमानः सततं ममेित ।
िवज्ञानकोशोऽयमितप्रकाशः प्रकृ ष्टसािन्नध्यवशात्परात्मनः ।
अतो भवत्येष उपािधरस्य यदात्मधीः संसरित भ्रमेण ॥ १८८ ॥

**Transliteration**

bhuṅkte vicitrāsvapi yoniṣu vrajan
nāyāti niryātyadha ūrdhvameṣaḥ |
asyaiva vijñānamāyāsya jāgrat
svapnādyavasthāḥ sukhaduḥkhabhogaḥ || 187 ||

**Translation**

“It comes and goes up and down, taking birth in various bodies. Waking, dream and other states of consciousness, and the experiences of joy and sorrow belong to this intellectual sheath.“
