## Text 68

**Sanskrit**

शृणुष्वाविहतो िवद्वन्यन्मया समुदीयर्ते ।
तदेतच्छ्रवणात्सद्यो भवबन्धािद्वमोक्ष्यसे ॥ ६८ ॥

**Transliteration**

śṛṇuṣvāvahito vidvanyanmayā samudīryate |
tadetacchravaṇātsadyo bhavabandhādvimokṣyase || 68 ||

**Translation**

Listen attentively, O learned one, to what I shall now tell you. Hearing it, you shall immediately be liberated from the bondage of worldly existence.”
