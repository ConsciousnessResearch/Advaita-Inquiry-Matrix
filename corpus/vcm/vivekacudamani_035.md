## Text 35

**Sanskrit**

स्वािमन्नमस्ते नतलोकबन्धो
कारुण्यिसन्धो पिततं भवाब्धौ ।
मामुद्धरात्मीयकटाक्षदृष्ट्या
ऋज्व्याितकारुण्यसुधािभवृष्ट्या ॥ ३५ ॥

**Transliteration**

svāminnamaste natalokabandho
kāruṇyasindho patitaṃ bhavābdhau |
māmuddharātmīyakaṭākṣadṛṣṭyā
ṛjvyātikāruṇyasudhābhivṛṣṭyā || 35 ||

**Translation**

“O Master, O kinsman of all who reverently surrender unto Thee, Thou ocean of mercy, I salute Thee; save me, fallen as I am into this sea of worldly existence, with a direct glance from Thine eyes which shower the nectarean Grace Supreme.”
