## Text 42

**Sanskrit**

िवद्वान् स तस्मा उपसित्तमीयुषे
मुमुक्षवे साधु यथोक्तकािरणे ।
प्रशान्तिचत्ताय शमािन्वताय
तत्त्वोपदेशं कृ पयैव कु यार्त् ॥ ४२ ॥

**Transliteration**

vidvān sa tasmā upasattimīyuṣe
mumukṣave sādhu yathoktakāriṇe |
praśāntacittāya śamānvitāya
tattvopadeśaṃ kṛpayaiva kuryāt || 42 ||

**Translation**

To him who has sought his protection, thirsting for Liberation, who duly obeys the injunctions of the Scriptures, who is of serene mind endowed with calmness, the sage proceeds to inculcate the truth out of sheer grace.
