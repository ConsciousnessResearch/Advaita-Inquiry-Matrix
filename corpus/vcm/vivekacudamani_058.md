## Text 58

**Sanskrit**

वाग्वैखरी शब्दझरी शास्त्रव्याख्यानकौशलम् ।
वैदुष्यं िवदुषां तद्वद्भुक्तये न तु मुक्तये ॥ ५८ ॥

**Transliteration**

vāgvaikharī śabdajharī śāstravyākhyānakauśalam |
vaiduṣyaṃ viduṣāṃ tadvadbhuktaye na tu muktaye || 58 ||

**Translation**

“Loud speech in a stream of words, efﬁciency in commenting upon the scriptures, erudition—these bring only a little material satisfaction to the scholar; they are insufﬁcient to bring about liberation.”
