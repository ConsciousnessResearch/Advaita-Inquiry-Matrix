## Text 161

**Sanskrit**

अत्रात्मबुिद्धं  त्यज मूढबुद्धे
त्वङ् मांसमेदोऽिस्थपुरीषराशौ ।
सवार्त्मिन ब्रह्मिण िनिवर् कल्पे
कु रुष्व शािन्त परमां भजस्व ॥ १६१ ॥

**Transliteration**

atrātmabuddhiṃ tyaja mūḍhabuddhe
tvaṅmāṃsamedo'sthipurīṣarāśau |
sarvātmani brahmaṇi nirvikalpe
kuruṣva śānti paramāṃ bhajasva || 161 ||

**Translation**

“O thou of stupid intellect! Give up identifying Ātman with this body made of skin, ﬂesh, fat, bones and excreta. Instead, rest your mind on Brahman, the one Ātman of all, which is not subject to change. And then, enjoy supreme bliss.“
