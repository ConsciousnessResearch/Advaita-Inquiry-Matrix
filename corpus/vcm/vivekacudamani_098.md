## Text 98

**Sanskrit**

कत्रार्िदभावं प्रितपद्य राजते यत्र स्वयं भाित ह्ययं परात्मा ।
धीमात्रकोपािधरशेषसाक्षी न िलप्यते तत्कृ तकमर्लेशैः
यस्मादसङ्गस्तत एव कमर्िभः
न िलप्यते िकं िचदुपािधना कृ तैः ॥ ९९ ॥

**Transliteration**

svapno bhavatyasya vibhaktyavasthā
svamātraśeṣeṇa vibhāti yatra |
svapne tu buddhiḥ svayameva jāgrat-
kālīnanānāvidhavāsanābhiḥ || 98 ||

**Translation**

“Dream is the state when the subtle body is distinctly in expression, where it expresses all by itself. In dream, the intellect by itself revels as the agent of experiences etc., due to the various impressions it gathered during the waking state.“
