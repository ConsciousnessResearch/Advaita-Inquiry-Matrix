## Text 14

**Sanskrit**

अिधकािरणमाशास्ते फलिसिद्धिवर् शेषतः ।
उपाया देशकालाद्याः सन्त्यिस्मन्सहकािरणः ॥ १४ ॥

**Transliteration**

adhikāriṇamāśāste phalasiddhirviśeṣataḥ |
upāyā deśakālādyāḥ santyasminsahakāriṇaḥ || 14 ||

**Translation**

Success depends mainly on the qualiﬁcations of the aspirant; time, place and other such means are but auxiliaries in this regard.
