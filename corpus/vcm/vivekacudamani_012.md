## Text 12

**Sanskrit**

सम्यिग्वचारतः िसद्धा रज्जुतत्त्वावधारणा ।
भ्रान्तोिदतमहासपर्भयदुःखिवनािशनी ॥ १२ ॥

**Transliteration**

samyagvicārataḥ siddhā rajjutattvāvadhāraṇā |
bhrāntoditamahāsarpabhayaduḥkhavināśinī || 12 ||

**Translation**

The conviction of the reality of the rope, gained by adequate reasoning, ends the great fear and misery caused by the snake, worked up in the deluded mind.
