## Text 75

**Sanskrit**

य एषु मूढा िवषयेषु बद्धा
रागोरुपाशेन सुदुदर्मेन ।
आयािन्त िनयार्न्त्यध ऊध्वर्मुच्चैः
स्वकमर्दूतेन जवेन नीताः ॥ ७५ ॥

**Transliteration**

ya eṣu mūḍhā viṣayeṣu baddhā
rāgorupāśena sudurdamena |
āyānti niryāntyadha ūrdhvamuccaiḥ
svakarmadūtena javena nītāḥ || 75 ||

**Translation**

“Those thoughtless ones who are bound to these sense-objects by the stout ropes of attachment so very difﬁcult to cut asunder, come and go, carried up and down by the compelling force of the envoy of the reactions of their own past actions.“
