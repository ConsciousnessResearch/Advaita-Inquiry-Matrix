## Text 10

**Sanskrit**

संन्यस्य सवर्कमार्िण भवबन्धिवमुक्तये ।
यत्यतां पिण्डतैधीर्रैरात्माभ्यास उपिस्थतैः ॥ १० ॥

**Transliteration**

saṃnyasya sarvakarmāṇi bhavabandhavimuktaye |
yatyatāṃ paṇḍitairdhīrairātmābhyāsa upasthitaiḥ || 10 ||

**Translation**

Let the wise and erudite man, commencing the practice of realization of Ātman, renounce all works and cut loose the bonds of birth and death.
