## Text 4

**Sanskrit**

लब्ध्वा कथंिचन्नरजन्म दुलर्भं
ंस्त्वं श्रुितपारदशर्नम् ।
तत्रािप पु
यस्त्वात्ममुक्तौ न यतेत मूढधीः
स ह्यात्महा स्वं िविनहन्त्यसद्ग्रहात् ॥ ४ ॥

**Transliteration**

labdhvā kathaṃcinnarajanma durlabhaṃ
tatrāpi puṃstvaṃ śrutipāradarśanam |
yastvātmamuktau na yateta mūḍhadhīḥ
sa hyātmahā svaṃ vinihantyasadgrahāt || 4 ||

**Translation**

The man who, having somehow obtained a human birth with a male body and mastery of the Vedas to boot, is so foolish not to exert himself for Self- realization, verily commits suicide— for he kills himself by clinging to things unreal.
