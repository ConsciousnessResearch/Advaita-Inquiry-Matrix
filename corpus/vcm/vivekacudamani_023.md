## Text 23

**Sanskrit**

िवषयेभ्यः परावत्यर् स्थापनं स्वस्वगोलके  ।
उभयेषािमिन्द्रयाणां स दमः पिरकीितर् तः ।
बाह्यानालम्बनं वृत्तेरेषोपरितरुत्तमा ॥ २३ ॥

**Transliteration**

viṣayebhyaḥ parāvartya sthāpanaṃ svasvagolake |
ubhayeṣāmindriyāṇāṃ sa damaḥ parikīrtitaḥ
bāhyānālambanaṃ vṛttereṣoparatiruttamā || 23 ||

**Translation**

Turning both kinds of sense-organs away from sense objects, and placing them in their respective centers of activity is called dama or self-control. The best uparati or self-withdrawal consists in the thought-waves ceasing to act from the inﬂuence of external objects.
