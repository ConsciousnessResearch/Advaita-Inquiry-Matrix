## Text 129

**Sanskrit**

यस्य सिन्निधमात्रेण देहेिन्द्रयमनोिधयः ।
िवषयेषु स्वकीयेषु वतर्न्ते प्रेिरता इव ॥ १२९ ॥

**Transliteration**

yasya sannidhimātreṇa dehendriyamanodhiyaḥ |
viṣayeṣu svakīyeṣu vartante preritā iva || 129 ||

**Translation**

“Simply by whose presence, the body, senses, mind and intellect act in their spheres as if impelled thereto—that is the Ātman.
