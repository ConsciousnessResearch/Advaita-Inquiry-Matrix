## Text 103

**Sanskrit**

अन्तःकरणमेतेषु चक्षुरािदषु वष्मर्िण ।
अहिमत्यिभमानेन ितष्ठत्याभासतेजसा ॥ १०३ ॥

**Transliteration**

antaḥkaraṇameteṣu cakṣurādiṣu varṣmaṇi |
ahamityabhimānena tiṣṭhatyābhāsatejasā || 103 ||

**Translation**

“The antaḥkaraṇa has its seat in the eye and other organs of the body, identifying itself with them with the sense of ‘I’ by reﬂection of the Ātman in it.
