## Text 138

**Sanskrit**

अतिस्मं स्तद्बुिद्धः प्रभवित िवमूढस्य तमसा
िववेकाभावाद्वै स्फु रित भुजगे रज्जुिधषणा ।
ततोऽनथर्व्रातो िनपतित समादातुरिधकः
ततो योऽसद्ग्राहः स िह भवित बन्धः शृणु सखे ॥ १३८ ॥

**Transliteration**

atasmiṃstadbuddhiḥ prabhavati vimūḍhasya tamasā
vivekābhāvādvai sphurati bhujage rajjudhiṣaṇā |
tato'narthavrāto nipatati samādāturadhikaḥ
tato yo'sadgrāhaḥ sa hi bhavati bandhaḥ śṛṇu sakhe || 138 ||

**Translation**

“By ajñāna, which is of the nature of tamas, an undiscriminating man mistakes what is not a thing as that thing. This is due to lack of discrimination, like thinking a serpent to be a rope. Then great calamities befall one subject to it. Hence, listen, my friend! Mistaking not-Self for the Self is what constitutes bondage.“
