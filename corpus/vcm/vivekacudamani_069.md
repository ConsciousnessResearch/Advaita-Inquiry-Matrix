## Text 69

**Sanskrit**

मोक्षस्य हेतुः प्रथमो िनगद्यते
वैराग्यमत्यन्तमिनत्यवस्तुषु ।
ततः शमश्चािप दमिस्तितक्षा
न्यासः प्रसक्तािखलकमर्णां भृशम् ॥ ६९ ॥

**Transliteration**

mokṣasya hetuḥ prathamo nigadyate
vairāgyamatyantamanityavastuṣu |
tataḥ śamaścāpi damastitikṣā
nyāsaḥ prasaktākhilakarmaṇāṃ bhṛśam || 69 ||

**Translation**

“The ﬁrst step to liberation is complete detachment from impermanent things. Then follow calmness, self-control, forbearance, and complete renunciation of all selﬁsh actions.“
