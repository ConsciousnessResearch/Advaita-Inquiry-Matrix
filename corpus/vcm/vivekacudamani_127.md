## Text 127

**Sanskrit**

यः पश्यित स्वयं सवर्ं यं न पश्यित कश्चन ।
यश्चेतयित बुद्ध्यािद न तद्यं चेतयत्ययम् ॥ १२७ ॥

**Transliteration**

yaḥ paśyati svayaṃ sarvaṃ yaṃ na paśyati kaścana |
yaścetayati buddhyādi na tadyaṃ cetayatyayam || 127 ||

**Translation**

“That which perceives everything, but which nothing can perceive; that which illumines the buddhi etc., but cannot Itself be illumined— that is the Ātman.
