## Text 105

**Sanskrit**

िवषयाणामानुकू ल्ये सुखी दुःखी िवपयर्ये ।
सुखं दुःखं च तद्धमर्ः सदानन्दस्य नात्मनः ॥ १०५ ॥

**Transliteration**

viṣayāṇāmānukūlye sukhī duḥkhī viparyaye |
sukhaṃ duḥkhaṃ ca taddharmaḥ sadānandasya nātmanaḥ || 105 ||

**Translation**

“When sense objects are favorable, one considers oneself happy, and unhappy if they are otherwise. Pleasure and suffering
