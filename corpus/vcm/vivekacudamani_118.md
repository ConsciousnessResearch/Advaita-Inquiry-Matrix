## Text 118

**Sanskrit**

िमश्रस्य सत्त्वस्य भविन्त धमार्ः
त्वमािनताद्या िनयमा यमाद्याः ।
श्रद्धा च भिक्तश्च मुमुक्षता च
दैवी च सम्पित्तरसिन्नवृित्तः ॥ ११८ ॥

**Transliteration**

miśrasya sattvasya bhavanti dharmāḥ
tvamānitādyā niyamā yamādyāḥ |
śraddhā ca bhaktiśca mumukṣatā ca
daivī ca sampattirasannivṛttiḥ || 118 ||

**Translation**

“The features of mixed sattva are the complete absence of pride, etc., the presence of yama-niyama etc., and faith, devotion, longing for liberation, the divine tendencies, and natural withdrawal from everything unreal.“
