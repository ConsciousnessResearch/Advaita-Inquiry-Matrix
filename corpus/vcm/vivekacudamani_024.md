## Text 24

**Sanskrit**

सहनं सवर्दुःखानामप्रतीकारपूवर्कम् ।
िचन्तािवलापरिहतं सा ितितक्षा िनगद्यते ॥ २४ ॥

**Transliteration**

sahanaṃ sarvaduḥkhānāmapratīkārapūrvakam |
cintāvilāparahitaṃ sā titikṣā nigadyate || 24 ||

**Translation**

Bearing all afﬂictions without caring to redress them, while remaining free from anxiety or lamentation
