## Text 120

**Sanskrit**

अव्यक्तमेतित्त्रगुणैिनर् रुक्तं
तत्कारणं नाम शरीरमात्मनः ।
सुषुिप्तरेतस्य िवभक्त्यवस्था
प्रलीनसवेर्िन्द्रयबुिद्धवृित्तः ॥ १२० ॥

**Transliteration**

avyaktametattriguṇairniruktaṃ
tatkāraṇaṃ nāma śarīramātmanaḥ |
suṣuptiretasya vibhaktyavasthā
pralīnasarvendriyabuddhivṛttiḥ || 120 ||

**Translation**

“This Unmanifested is said to be made of three guṇas. It is the Ātman’s Causal Body. Suṣupti consciousness is a distinct aspect of it, and is characterised by the dissolution of the activities of all sense organs and buddhi.“
