## Text 108

**Sanskrit**

सन्नाप्यसन्नाप्युभयाित्मका नो
िभन्नाप्यिभन्नाप्युभयाित्मका नो ।
साङ्गाप्यनङ्गा ह्युभयाित्मका नो
महाद्भुतािनवर्चनीयरूपा ॥ १०९ ॥

**Transliteration**

avyaktanāmnī parameśaśaktiḥ
anādyavidyā triguṇātmikā parā |
kāryānumeyā sudhiyaiva māyā
yayā jagatsarvamidaṃ prasūyate || 108 ||

**Translation**

“Māyā is called avyakta. It is the power of Parameśvara. It is beginningless avidyā. It is composed of three guṇas. It is superior to its effects, and is to be inferred from them by the wise
