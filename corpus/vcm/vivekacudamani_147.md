## Text 147

**Sanskrit**

छेत्तु
नास्त्रैनर् शस्त्रैरिनलेन विन्हना
ं न शक्यो न च कमर्कोिटिभः ।
िववेकिवज्ञानमहािसना िवना
धातुः प्रसादेन िशतेन मञ्जुना ॥ १४७ ॥

**Transliteration**

nāstrairna śastrairanilena vanhinā
chettuṃ na śakyo na ca karmakoṭibhiḥ |
vivekavijñānamahāsinā vinā
dhātuḥ prasādena śitena mañjunā || 147 ||

**Translation**

“Neither by ordinary nor divine weapons, nor by wind, nor by ﬁre, nor by tens of millions of actions, can this bondage be destroyed. Nothing except the mind, sharpened by the great sword of Knowledge —discrimination given by the Grace of the Good Lord —can one end this bondage.“
