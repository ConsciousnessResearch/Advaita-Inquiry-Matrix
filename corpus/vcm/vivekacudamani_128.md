## Text 128

**Sanskrit**

येन िवश्विमदं व्याप्तं यं न व्याप्नोित िकं चन ।
अभारूपिमदं सवर्ं यं भान्त्यमनुभात्ययम् ॥ १२८ ॥

**Transliteration**

yena viśvamidaṃ vyāptaṃ yaṃ na vyāpnoti kiṃcana |
abhārūpamidaṃ sarvaṃ yaṃ bhāntyamanubhātyayam || 128 ||

**Translation**

“That by which this universe is enveloped, which no object whatsoever envelops; that effulgent one on which all this universe of the form of non-effulgence depends for its shining—that is the Ātman.
