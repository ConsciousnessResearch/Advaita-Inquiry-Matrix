## Text 26

**Sanskrit**

सवर्दा स्थापनं बुद्धेः शुद्धे ब्रह्मिण सवर्दा ।
तत्समाधानिमत्युक्तं न तु िचत्तस्य लालनम् ॥ २६ ॥

**Transliteration**

sarvadā sthāpanaṃ buddheḥ śuddhe brahmaṇi sarvadā |
tatsamādhānamityuktaṃ na tu cittasya lālanam || 26 ||

**Translation**

Samādhāna or one-pointedness is constant concentration of the intellect on the ever-pure Brahman; it is not indulgence of the curious mind.
