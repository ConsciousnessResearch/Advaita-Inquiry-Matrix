## Text 61

**Sanskrit**

अज्ञानसपर्दष्टस्य ब्रह्मज्ञानौषधं िवना ।
िकमु वेदैश्च शास्त्रैश्च िकमु मन्त्रैः िकमौषधैः ॥ ६१ ॥

**Transliteration**

ajñānasarpadaṣṭasya brahmajñānauṣadhaṃ vinā |
kimu vedaiśca śāstraiśca kimu mantraiḥ kimauṣadhaiḥ || 61 ||

**Translation**

“For one who has been bitten by the serpent of ignorance, the only remedy is knowledge of Brahman. Of what use are the Vedas and the scriptures, mantras and medicines to such a victim of poison?”
