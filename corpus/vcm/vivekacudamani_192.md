## Text 192

**Sanskrit**

िशष्य उवाच । भ्रमेणाप्यन्यथा वास्तु जीवभावः परात्मनः ।
तदुपाधेरनािदत्वान्नानादेनार्श इष्यते ॥ १९२ ॥

**Transliteration**

śiṣya uvāca | bhrameṇāpyanyathā vāstu jīvabhāvaḥ parātmanaḥ |
tadupādheranāditvānnānādernāśa iṣyate || 192 ||

**Translation**

The disciple asked: That the Supreme Self has come to consider Itself as the embodied self, through delusion or otherwise, is a superimposition which is beginningless; that which is beginningless cannot be said to have an end.
