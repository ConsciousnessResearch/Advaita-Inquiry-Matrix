## Text 123

**Sanskrit**

माया मायाकायर्ं सवर्ं महदािददेहपयर्न्तम् ।
असिददमनात्मतत्त्वं िविद्ध त्वं मरुमरीिचकाकल्पम् ॥ १२३ ॥

**Transliteration**

māyā māyākāryaṃ sarvaṃ mahadādidehaparyantam |
asadidamanātmatattvaṃ viddhi tvaṃ marumarīcikākalpam || 123 ||

**Translation**

“Know that all these—Māyā, and its effects from the mahat up to the body—are asat
