## Text 15

**Sanskrit**

अतो िवचारः कतर्व्यो िजज्ञासोरात्मवस्तुनः ।
ं गुरुं  ब्रह्मिवदुत्तमम् ॥ १५ ॥
समासाद्य दयािसन्धु

**Transliteration**

ato vicāraḥ kartavyo jijñāsorātmavastunaḥ |
samāsādya dayāsindhuṃ guruṃ brahmaviduttamam || 15 ||

**Translation**

Hence the seeker after the Reality of the Ātman should take to inquiry, after duly approaching the Guru—the best of the knowers of Brahman, and an ocean of mercy.
