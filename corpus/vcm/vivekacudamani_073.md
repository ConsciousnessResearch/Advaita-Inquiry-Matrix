## Text 73

**Sanskrit**

परस्परांशैिमर् िलतािन भूत्वा
स्थूलािन च स्थूलशरीरहेतवः ।
मात्रास्तदीया िवषया भविन्त
शब्दादयः पञ्च सुखाय भोक्तुः ॥ ७४ ॥

**Transliteration**

ahaṃmametiprathitaṃ śarīraṃ
mohāspadaṃ sthūlamitīryate budhaiḥ |
nabhonabhasvaddahanāmbubhūmayaḥ
sūkṣmāṇi bhūtāni bhavanti tāni || 73 ||

**Translation**

“This body, the seat of delusion, expressing in terms of ‘I’ and ‘mine,’ is termed by the wise as the gross body. Space, air, ﬁre, water, and earth are the subtle elements.“
