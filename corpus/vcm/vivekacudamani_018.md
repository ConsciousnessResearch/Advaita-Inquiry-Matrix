## Text 18

**Sanskrit**

साधनान्यत्र चत्वािर किथतािन मनीिषिभः ।
येषु सत्स्वेव सिन्नष्ठा यदभावे न िसध्यित ॥ १८ ॥

**Transliteration**

sādhanānyatra catvāri kathitāni manīṣibhiḥ |
yeṣu satsveva sanniṣṭhā yadabhāve na sidhyati || 18 ||

**Translation**

Great sages have spoken of four qualiﬁcations— which, when present, one succeeds in realization of Brahman, and in the absence of which the goal is not attained.
