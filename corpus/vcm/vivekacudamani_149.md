## Text 149

**Sanskrit**

कोशैरन्नमयाद्यैः पञ्चिभरात्मा न संवृतो भाित ।
िनजशिक्तसमुत्पन्नैः शैवालपटलैिरवाम्बु वापीस्थम् ॥ १४९ ॥

**Transliteration**

kośairannamayādyaiḥ pañcabhirātmā na saṃvṛto bhāti |
nijaśaktisamutpannaiḥ śaivālapaṭalairivāmbu vāpīstham || 149 ||

**Translation**

“The Ātman does not shine clearly, being covered by the ﬁve food and other sheaths which have arisen by its own power, like the water in a well covered by a sheet of moss, which is born out of itself.”
