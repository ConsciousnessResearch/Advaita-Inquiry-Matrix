## Text 144

**Sanskrit**

एताभ्यामेव शिक्तभ्यां बन्धः पु
ंसः समागतः ।
याभ्यां िवमोिहतो देहं मत्वात्मानं भ्रमत्ययम् ॥ १४४ ॥

**Transliteration**

etābhyāmeva śaktibhyāṃ bandhaḥ puṃsaḥ samāgataḥ |
yābhyāṃ vimohito dehaṃ matvātmānaṃ bhramatyayam || 144 ||

**Translation**

“Constrained by these two powers, deluded by them, subject to bondage, a man mistaking the body to be the Ātman wanders about in such delusion.”
