## Text 159

**Sanskrit**

देहोऽहिमत्येव जडस्य बुिद्धः
देहे च जीवे िवदुषस्त्वहंधीः ।
िववेकिवज्ञानवतो महात्मनो
ब्रह्माहिमत्येव मितः सदात्मिन ॥ १६० ॥

**Transliteration**

tvaṅmāṃsamedo'sthipurīṣarāśā
vahaṃmatiṃ mūḍhajanaḥ karoti |
vilakṣaṇaṃ vetti vicāraśīlo
nijasvarūpaṃ paramārtha bhūtam || 159 ||

**Translation**

“The fool identiﬁes Ātman with his body made of skin, ﬂesh, fat, bones and excreta. The man of discrimination, however, knows his true nature to be the Supreme Reality.“
