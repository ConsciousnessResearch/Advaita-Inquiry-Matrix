## Text 101

**Sanskrit**

उच्छ्वासिनःश्वासिवजृम्भणक्षुत्
प्रस्यन्दनाद्युत्क्रमणािदकाः िक्रयाः ।
प्राणािदकमार्िण वदिन्त तज्ञाः
प्राणस्य धमार्वशनािपपासे ॥ १०२ ॥

**Transliteration**

andhatvamandatvapaṭutvadharmāḥ
sauguṇyavaiguṇyavaśāddhi cakṣuṣaḥ |
bādhiryamūkatvamukhāstathaiva
śrotrādidharmā na tu vetturātmanaḥ || 101 ||

**Translation**

“The qualities of blindness, defective and clear vision belong to the eye because of its healthy or diseased condition. Similarly, qualities like deafness, dumbness, etc. pertain to the ear etc., but they do not belong to the Ātman, which is always the witness.“
