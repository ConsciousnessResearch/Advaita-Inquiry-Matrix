## Text 171

**Sanskrit**

सुषुिप्तकाले मनिस प्रलीने
नैवािस्त िकं िचत्सकलप्रिसद्धेः ।
अतो मनःकिल्पतेव पु
ंसः
संसार एतस्य न वस्तुतोऽिस्त ॥ १७१ ॥

**Transliteration**

suṣuptikāle manasi pralīne
naivāsti kiṃcitsakalaprasiddheḥ |
ato manaḥkalpiteva puṃsaḥ
saṃsāra etasya na vastuto'sti || 171 ||

**Translation**

“It is known by all that there is nothing in dreamless sleep when the mind is stilled. Hence saṃsāra is a creation of the mind. It does not exist in reality.“
