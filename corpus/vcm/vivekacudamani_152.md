## Text 152

**Sanskrit**

आत्मानात्मिववेकः कतर्व्यो बन्धमुक्तये िवदुषा ।
तेनैवानन्दी भवित स्वं िवज्ञाय सिच्चदानन्दम् ॥ १५२ ॥

**Transliteration**

ātmānātmavivekaḥ kartavyo bandhamuktaye viduṣā |
tenaivānandī bhavati svaṃ vijñāya saccidānandam || 152 ||

**Translation**

ātmānātmavivekaḥ—discrimination of the Self from the non-Self; kartavyaḥ —should be done; bandhamuktaye—for liberation from bondage to
