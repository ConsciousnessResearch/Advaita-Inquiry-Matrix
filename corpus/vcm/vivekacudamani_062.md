## Text 62

**Sanskrit**

न गच्छित िवना पानं व्यािधरौषधशब्दतः ।
िवनापरोक्षानुभवं ब्रह्मशब्दैनर् मुच्यते ॥ ६२ ॥

**Transliteration**

na gacchati vinā pānaṃ vyādhirauṣadhaśabdataḥ |
vināparokṣānubhavaṃ brahmaśabdairna mucyate || 62 ||

**Translation**

“A disease is not cured by merely repeating the name of the medicine, without taking it. Similarly, without direct realization, none can be liberated by a mere utterance of the word ‘Brahman’.”
