## Text 107

**Sanskrit**

यत्सुषुप्तौ िनिवर् षय आत्मानन्दोऽनुभूयते ।
श्रुितः प्रत्यक्षमैितह्यमनुमानं च जाग्रित ॥ १0७ ॥

**Transliteration**

yatsuṣuptau nirviṣaya ātmānando'nubhūyate |
śrutiḥ pratyakṣamaitihyamanumānaṃ ca jāgrati || 107 ||

**Translation**

In dreamless sleep there are no sense objects; the bliss of Ātman is experienced then. This is attested by śruti, sense perception, tradition and inference, in the waking state.
