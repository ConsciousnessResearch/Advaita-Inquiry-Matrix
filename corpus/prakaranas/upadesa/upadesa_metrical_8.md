**Sanskrit**

चचनतस्र्रूपे स्र्त एर् मे मते िसाददयोगस्तर् मोहकारितः |
अतो न क्रक ं चचत्तर् चेवष्टतेन मे फलं भर्ेत्सर्ववर्शेषहानतः ||१||

**Transliteration**

citisvarūpe svata eva me mate rasādiyogastava mohakāritaḥ |
ato na kiṁcittava ceṣṭitena me phalaṁ bhavetsarvaviśeṣahānataḥ ||1||

**Translation**

The connection of enjoyment etc., with me, O My mind, who am by nature Consciousness Itself is due to the delusion created by you. As I am free from all attributes there is no utility accruing to Me from your efforts.

**Sanskrit**

वर्मुच्य मायामयकायवताममह प्रशाग्न्तमायाह्यसदीदहतात्सदा |
अहं पिंब्रह्म सदा ग्व्कमुक्र्त्तथाजमेक ं द्वयर्ग्जवतं यथः ||२||

**Transliteration**

vimucya māyāmayakāryatāmiha praśāntimāyāhyasadīhitātsadā |
ahaṁ paraṁbrahma sadā vkimuktavattathājamekaṁ dvayavarjitaṁ yathaḥ ||2||

**Translation**

Give up false attempts and come to rest in Me from constant vain efforts as I am always the supreme Brahman as if free from bondage, unborn and devoid of duality.

**Sanskrit**

सदा च भूतेषु समोSग्स्म के र्लो यथा च खं सर्वगमक्षिं मशर्ं |
ननिन्तिं ननष्कलमक्रियं पिं ततो न मेSस्तीह फलं तर्ेदहतैः ||३||

**Transliteration**

sadā ca bhūteṣu samo’smi kevalo yathā ca khaṁ sarvagamakṣaraṁ śivaṁ |
nirantaraṁ niṣkalamakriyaṁ paraṁ tato na me’stīha phalaṁ tavehitaiḥ ||3||

**Translation**

 The supreme Brahman, the same in all beings and free from all attributes I am all-pervading like the ether, imperishable, auspicious, homogeneous, partless and actionless. I, therefore, have no benefit to be derived from your efforts.

**Sanskrit**

अहं ममैको न मदन्यददष्यते तथा न कस्याप्यहमस्म्यसंगतः |
असङ्गरूपोSहमतो न मे त्र्या क ृ तेन कायं तर् चाद्वयत्र्तः ||४||

**Transliteration**

ahaṁ mamaiko na madanyadiṣyate tathā na kasyāpyahamasmyasaṁgataḥ |
asaṅgarūpo’hamato na me tvayā kr̥tena kāryaṁ tava cādvayatvataḥ ||4||

**Translation**

No one different from Me can belong to Me who am one only. Nor can I who am unattached belong to anybody. I have, therefore, no benefit to be derived from anything done by you. As you are not other than Myself you can have no effort nor its results.

**Sanskrit**

फले च हेतौ च जनो वर्षक्र्ानननत प्रचचन्त्याहमतो वर्मोक्षिे |
जनस्य संर्ादमममं प्रक्लृप्तर्ान्स्र्रूपतत्त्र्ाथववर्बोधकाििम ् ||५||

**Transliteration**

phale ca hetau ca jano viṣaktavāniti pracintyāhamato vimokṣaṇe |
janasya saṁvādamimaṁ praklr̥ptavānsvarūpatattvārthavibodhakāraṇam ||5||

**Translation**

Considering that people are attached to the ideas of cause and effect, I have composed this dialogue (between the mind and the Self) leading to the understanding of the real nature of the Self in order that they might get freed from this (bondage).

**Sanskrit**

संर्ादमेतं यदद चचन्तयेन्निो वर्मुच्यतेSज्ञानमहाभयागमात ् |

**Transliteration**

**Translation**

A man gets liberated from Ignorance, the cause of great fear, and roams (over the world) free from desires, free from grief, a Knower of the Self, the same in all beings and happy if he ponders over this dialogue.
