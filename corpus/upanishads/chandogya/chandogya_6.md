## Text 6.1.1

**Sanskrit**

श्वेतकेतुर्हारुणेय आस तं ह पितोवाच श्वेतकेतो वस ब्रह्मचर्यं 
न वै सोम्यास्मत्कुलीनोऽननूच्य ब्रह्मबन्धुरिव भवतीति ॥ ६.१.१ ॥

**Transliteration**

śvetaketurhāruṇeya āsa taṃ ha pitovāca śvetaketo vasa brahmacaryaṃ 
na vai somyāsmatkulīno'nanūcya brahmabandhuriva bhavatīti || 6.1.1 ||

**Translation**

Āruṇi had a son named Śvetaketu. Once Āruṇi told him: ‘Śvetaketu, you should now live as a brahmacārin. No one in our family has not studied the scriptures and has not been a good brāhmin.’

## Text 6.1.3

**Sanskrit**

**Translation**
